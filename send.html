Small parcels

These are packages that weigh from fifty kilograms or less, and the transportation fee is one dollar per kilogram,
not including customs clearance.

Medium packages

These are parcels that weigh from fifty kilograms to two hundred kilograms, and the transportation fee is three dollars per kilogram,
not including customs clearance.

Large parcels

These are packages that weigh from two hundred kilograms and above, and the transportation fee is five dollars per kilogram,
not including customs clearance.
